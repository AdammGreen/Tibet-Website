His Holiness Dalai Lama

The Dalai Lama was born in 1935 in Taktser, China . When the Dalai Lama was 15, he took political power of Tibet as the Dalai Lama and the same year The People's Republic of China invaded . The Dalai Lama fled the world heritage site Potala Palace fearing his kidnapping and assassination , fled with thousands of followers to Himachal Pradesh in Dharamshala in northern India . The Dalai Lama and his followers established a government for the Tibetans in exile . The Dalai Lama has since taken numerous actions in desire of establishing a freer Tibetan state within the People's Republic of China. To his dismay, the Chinese government has shown no signs of moving toward peace and reconciliation with Tibet.

The Dalai Lama is a phenomenal man and is given huge respect across the political, scientific, health and environmental heavy weights of the world. The Dalai Lama has written over 130 books, conducted hundreds of conferences, university lectures and workshops worldwide, as part of his humanitarian efforts. He has been granted dozens of honorary doctorates and awards for his leadership of the Tibetan community, including the Nobel Peace Prize in 1989.

Buddhists believe in the theory of reincarnation and the Dalai Lama is believed by Buddhists to be able to choose the body into which he is reincarnated. His reincarnation is then found and then becomes the next Dalai Lama. The Dalai Lamas are believed by many Buddhists to be the manifestation of Chenrezig , the Bodhisattva of Compassion and the patron saint of Tibet. Bodhisattvas are realized beings, inspired by the wish to attain complete enlightenment, who have vowed to be reborn in the world to help all living beings. Dalia Lama's Website

Chenrezig, also known as Avalokitesvara

It is the responsibility of the High Lamas (spiritual teachers) of the Gelugpa lineage and the Tibetan government to seek out and find the next Dalai Lama following the death of the Dalai Lama. The search is generally limited to Tibet, although the current Dalai Lama has said that there is a chance that he will not be reborn, and that if he is, it would not be in a country under Chinese rule . During the search the High Lamas may have visions or dreams and try to find signs. In the instance of the previous Dalai Lama being cremated, they may watch the direction of the smoke to suggest where the rebirth will take place.

When a successor is found, a series of tests believed to ensure that they are the genuine reincarnation of the previous Dalai Lama are performed. The Lamas assess the candidate against a set of criteria. Most importantly the Lamas will present the child with various objects to see if they can identify those which belonged to the previous Dalai Lama. Once the Dalai Lama has been identified, the High Lamas will report to important individuals and to the Government that the Dalai Lama has been found.
